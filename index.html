Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How long does a custom furniture order take?

+

Simple items take 5–10 days; larger projects like bedroom sets or cabinets take 2–4 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline when you order.

Do you deliver outside Enugu?

+

Yes! We deliver to other states in Nigeria. Out-of-state delivery fees are quoted based on distance and item size.

Can I bring my own design or photos?

+

Absolutely! Bring sketches, photos, or Pinterest boards. Our craftsmen will bring your exact vision to life.

What types of wood do you use?

+

We work with mahogany, iroko, teak, oak, and engineered wood options for more budget-friendly pieces.

Do you repair old furniture?

+

Yes! We offer full repair and restoration — re-upholstery, wood repairs, repainting, and staining. Send photos for a free estimate.

How do I place an order?

+

Fill the form above, call/WhatsApp us at 09090907023, or visit us at 2 Oba St, off Ogui Road, Achara, Enugu. Open Mon–Sat 7am–6pm.
